About Me

Hey there, and welcome to my Portfolio!

Let's rewind to 2022 – the year I stumbled upon my passion for software while crafting my own travel blog. It was like discovering a hidden treasure, and I knew I wanted to start a career in Tech.
Starting with the building blocks of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I soon found myself knee-deep in Java, crafting my very own Text-based Tamagotchi – a humble beginning to my coding journey.

Fast forward to late 2023, where I found myself in the middle of a renowned German company's Full-Stack Developer program.
With a roadmap stretching to early 2026, I'm on a thrilling ride through the realms of Java, Kotlin, Python, TKInter, Pandas, BigQuery and many more to come, soaking up the knowledge along the way.
But it doesn't stop there. I've got my sights set on Machine Learning and App Development, currently learning to work with Android Studio.
In the near future, you'll likely find me dipping my toes into the world of Game Development, propably diving into Godot.

When I'm not lost in lines of code, I'm strumming away on my guitar or immersing myself in the pixelated wonders of video games. Rock or Metal concerts and festivals are my jam, pulsating with energy and excitement.
As a little treat for the body, you'll catch me pedaling into nature on my bike or kicking around a football match with the crew.

Not to forget my peaceful hikes and camping trips – the perfect antidote to the hustle and bustle of the coding world.
